Vacuum of space, commonly found in low-Earth orbit

Appearance:

Simple circular shape which changes colors and gains rings surrounding its body once exposed to electricity, as well as growing in size when it eats other bacteria

Key Characteristics:

  • Circular shape
  • Eats green bacteria
  • Rings around body
  • Randomly generated colors

What we know so far about Cyberrona? The Cyberrona is a small, spacial bacteria belonging to the genus Corona. It is one of the only bacterias capable of enduring space's harsh environment. Whilst other bacterias have been discovered living in space, this is the most dynamic, complex organism found to date. It is carnivorous and presents predatory behaviors, enlarging in size after consuming other organisms. It appears to interact with electric currrents which give it rings which orbit its main body, as well as making it blink different colors and shake erratically when it does not move. It is still unknown whether the electricity is a benefit for the creature but it certainly does not diminish its normal capabilities.
Guidelines for human interaction with this creature Its recent discovery means that human interaction is not advised as we do not know the impact of the interaction would be. However, it does not appear to present aggressive behaviors towards the scientists examining it. It is also prohibited by government regulations to touch or entrap the creature as its effects on Earth could be detrimental to both national security and global health alike.
